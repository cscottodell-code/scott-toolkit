## Service Area

## Revenue Benchmarks

## Customer Segments

### Primary: Storm Damage Homeowner
- Homeowner in The Corridor
- Recently experienced storm damage (hail, wind, monsoon)
- Has homeowners insurance
- Overwhelmed, wants someone trustworthy to handle it

### Aspirational: All Homeowners
- Proactive maintenance (HVAC tune-ups, roof inspections, pest control)
- Home improvement projects
- Aging system replacements
- Annual home health checks

## Cross-Sell Opportunity Matrix

| If customer has... | They likely also need... |
|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Roof damage | Restoration, HVAC check, Pest control |
| Water damage | Roof repair, Packout, HVAC inspection |
| HVAC failure | Roof inspection, Pest control |
| Fire damage | Roof, HVAC, Packout, Pest control |
| Insurance claim | Public adjusting, All repair services |
| Pest problem | Roof inspection (entry points), HVAC check |
